                        <div class="content">
                            <span id="ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_ContentPlaceHolder1_ContentPlaceHolder1_Section01_ctl01_lblContent"><p>The investment is part of the EIB's new action plan for <strong>security and defense.</strong></p><div id='PolskieRadio_artykuly_intext_rec'><script>googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('PolskieRadio_artykuly_intext_rec'); });</script></div>
<p>Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk praised the agreement, stating that it represents a significant advancement in the plan to build an <strong>"iron dome"</strong> over <strong>Poland</strong> and <strong>Europe</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>"Today we can count on very cheap, favorable loans for investments that will serve civilian tasks and at the same time promote our defense and security,"</strong> Tusk said after his meeting with EIB President Calviño in Warsaw on Monday.</p>
<p>He emphasized the importance of the bank’s involvement in the <strong>Polish satellite program</strong>, which is being implemented in <strong>cooperation with France.</strong></p>

<p lang="en" dir="ltr">🇪🇺🇵🇱 Excellent meeting with Prime Minister <a href="https://twitter.com/donaldtusk?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">@donaldtusk</a>. Our priorities are fully aligned.<a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/Poland?src=hash&amp;ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">#Poland</a> is a great success story, a bright example of the benefits of EU membership.<br /><br />The <a href="https://twitter.com/EIB?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">@EIB</a> Group has invested €100bn and will be a key partner for security, growth &amp; social progress. <a href="https://t.co/rGPcudJkFg">pic.twitter.com/rGPcudJkFg</a></p>
— Nadia Calviño (@NadiaCalvino) <a href="https://twitter.com/NadiaCalvino/status/1792513675112087701?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">May 20, 2024</a></blockquote>
<p>
<script async="" src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script>
</p>
<p>Calviño noted that the investment marks the EIB's first action under its new security and defense plan. She recalled that the bank has already invested nearly <strong>EUR 100 billion in Poland</strong>, financing <strong>infrastructure</strong> projects such as <strong>roads</strong>, <strong>railways</strong>, <strong>energy transition</strong>, <strong>offshore wind energy</strong>, and the <strong>Warsaw metro.</strong></p>
<p><strong>"Poland is a great example of success, of taking advantage of the benefits of EU membership. We will always be a trusted partner of Poland to ensure prosperity and development," </strong>Calviño stated during her joint speech with Tusk.</p>
<p>Tusk expressed his gratitude to the EIB for its continued support, saying that the “EIB's readiness to support our plans for security, protection, and defense of Polish and European borders" was crucial for his country.</p>
