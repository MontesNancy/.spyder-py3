								Jakarta (ANTARA) - The Ministry of Industry is encouraging greater cooperation in the industrial sector with the Chinese government to make the supply chains of the two countries more competitive.<br />
<br />
&quot;We also encourage stronger cooperation between business players in Indonesia and business players in China to bolster not only things related to business development but also streamlining supply chains,&quot; the ministry&#39;s secretary general, Eko Cahyanto, said.<br />
<br />
He made the statement at the Indonesia-China Trade and Industrial Cooperation Seminar in Jakarta on Thursday.<br />
<br />
He noted that cooperation between Indonesia and China has been quite good in the past two decades.<br />
<br />
In the period from 2019 to 2023, China&#39;s investment in Indonesia reached US$28.4 billion, with the manufacturing sector attracting the biggest portion, or around 54 percent, of the investment from China.<br />
<br />
Cahyanto said that to boost cooperation with China, the Indonesian government has prepared incentives, both fiscal and non-fiscal, as well as adequate industrial human resources.<br />
<br />
&quot;The Ministry of Industry has also created quite a large number of industrial workers to support new investments, especially pioneer investments,&quot; he added.<br />
<br />
The government has prepared several tax relief policies for investors, including a tax holiday in the form of corporate income tax exemption for 5&ndash;20 years for pioneer industries with large and strategic investments.<br />
<br />
Furthermore, it is offering a tax allowance in the form of a reduction in company income tax, namely 30 percent of the investment value, for six years.<br />
<br />
It is also providing an exemption from import fees for two years for machinery, equipment, and raw materials.<br />
<br />
In addition, there is a super tax deduction of up to 200 percent for business players carrying out vocational activities and up to 300 percent for those carrying out research and development in Indonesia.<br />
