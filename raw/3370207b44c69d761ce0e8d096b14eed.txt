<h1 class="title-article">The Polish State Bank will finance investment projects in Ukraine.</h1>
<div class="today-date">Tuesday, May 21, 2024</div>
<div class="article-img">
<img data-lazyloaded="1" src="data:image/svg+xml;base64,PHN2ZyB4bWxucz0iaHR0cDovL3d3dy53My5vcmcvMjAwMC9zdmciIHdpZHRoPSI3MDciIGhlaWdodD0iNTAwIiB2aWV3Qm94PSIwIDAgNzA3IDUwMCI+PHJlY3Qgd2lkdGg9IjEwMCUiIGhlaWdodD0iMTAwJSIgZmlsbD0iI2VkZWRlZCIvPjwvc3ZnPg==" width="707" height="500" data-src="https://ubn.news/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/BGK.jpeg" alt="The Polish State Bank will finance investment projects in Ukraine." style="min-height:300px;" /><noscript><img width="707" height="500" src="https://ubn.news/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/BGK.jpeg" alt="The Polish State Bank will finance investment projects in Ukraine." style="min-height:300px;"/></noscript>
</div>
<div class="article-block">
<p>Bank Gospodarstwa Krajowego (BGK) plans to start providing loan guarantees for investment projects in Ukraine, <span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.paih.gov.pl/en/" data-wpel-link="external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow external noopener noreferrer">said</a></span> the Chairman of the Board of the Polish Investment Agency (PAIH) Andrzej Dycha.</p>
<p>&#8220;Implementation of the investment part of the Ukraine Facility program will begin in the third quarter of 2024, the funds will be transferred to authorized state institutions in EU countries&#8221;.</p>
<p>Dycha clarified that work is underway to change Polish legislation to enable BGK to use the Ukraine Facility program funds to reduce the cost of investment loans.</p>
<p>&#8220;We would like both Polish and Ukrainian enterprises to have access to these funds,&#8221; said Dycha.</p>
<p>The key Polish institution facilitating the establishment of contacts and the search for investment projects in Ukraine is the Kyiv representative office of PAIH.</p>
<p>&#8220;So far, more than 3,000 Polish companies have expressed their interest in working in Ukraine,&#8221; Dycha noted.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
